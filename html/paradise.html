The Paradise

Giaquinto, Corrado (1754 - 1757)

Between clouds and accompanied by angels, there are several rows of saints among which, in the center group, Saint Dominic, Saint Francis, Saint Thomas Aquinas, Saint Anthony of Padua and Saint Clare stand out, and on the right, Saint Agnes, Saint Cecilia and other holy martyrs. In the background, San Francisco de Paula, San Buenaventura (?) and other saints and, further away, the Magdalena and other holy women. It is a sketch for the dome of the Royal Chapel, to the right of the Trinity, and the coloring, even finer than that of its companions, shows an extraordinary delicacy in the second terms, with an exceptional range of pearly greys.
